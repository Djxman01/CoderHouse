Some Characters of Star Wars...

Kylo Ren

A dark side warrior with a mysterious past, Kylo Ren was neither Jedi nor Sith, but a product of both sides' teachings.

C-3PO

Built by Anakin Skywalker, C-3PO was designed as a protocol droid intended to assist in etiquette, customs, and translation

R2-D2

A reliable and versatile astromech droid, showing great bravery in rescuing his masters and their friends from many perils.

Stormtrooper

Stormtroopers are elite shock troops fanatically loyal to the Empire and impossible to sway from the Imperial cause.

Darth Vader

Darth Vader is a menacing villain. His head is covered by a mechanical helmet, and the sound of his breathing is an eerie, mechanical hiss.

Yoda

Yoda was a legendary Jedi Master and stronger than most in his connection with the Force.

Boba Fett

With his customized Mandalorian armor, deadly weaponry, Boba Fett was once regarded as one of the most fearsome and capable bounty hunters in the galaxy.

Rey Skywalker

Seemingly abandoned by her parents on Jakku, Rey grew up a scavenger amid the wreckage

Chewbacca

He is a Wookiee, a tall, hirsute, bipedal, intelligent species originating from the fictional planet of Kashyyyk.

Han Solo

Han Solo is a reckless smuggler with a sarcastic wit; he is "a very practical guy" and considers himself "a materialist"
